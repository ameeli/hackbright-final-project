Be present. Be happy. Wanda is here to help.

Unlike other animals, humans spend around half of the day thinking about something other than what is going on around at the moment, contemplating events that happened in the past, might happen in the future, or will never happen at all. When our minds wander in this way, we are less happy and perform worse in motor skill activities. Inspired by a recent study by Harvard psychology professor Daniel Gilbert, Wanda was developed to help you decrease your mind wandering and increase your happiness.
